FAQs_

How big can my team be?

The maximum team size allowed is 5.

Can I come individually?

No. A team of minimum 2 members is allowed.

When?

AREA' 51 2k22 will be a 3 stage hackathon with 1st 2 stages in online mode and the final stage will be conducted in offline manner on Apr 29th and 30th. 1st stage from Apr 16 10:00am - Apr 23 12:00am.2nd stage from Apr 23rd 2:00pm - Apr 27th 12:00am.

Where?

AREA' 51 finals will be conducted at Jyothi Engineering College,Cheruthuruthy,Kerala.

Will there be food arrangement?

Oh-Yeah-Absolutely. You will be provided with proper meals on both days. Thanks to our sponsors.

How much does it cost?

Everything at AREA' 51 is free of cost.

What are the criteria of shortlisting the teams?

The registered participants will be shortlisted based on their abstract for the 1st stage and for the 2nd stage based on their half prototype

What do I bring with me to the event?

You are required to bring your college ID, laptops, phone, chargers, extensions.

What is the eligibility criteria?

You must be a UG or PG student studying in any College in Kerala.
