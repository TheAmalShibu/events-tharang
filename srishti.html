EXPO 2022 RULES

  • The projects to be submitted can be based in any field.
  • The abstract of the project should be uploaded on or before 10th April 2022.
  • The event is conducted in 2 tracks viz. Circuit & Non- Circuit. All Electrical & Computer Science projects are to be included for the track 'Circuit'.All other projects including Civil & Mechanical domain are to be included under the track 'Non-Circuit'.
  • The event is exclusively meant for B.Tech/Diploma students only.
  • A team can have a maximum of 4 members, including the team leader.
  • Participants should submit the abstract of the idea via the google form at the time of registration.
  • Participants are also required to mention whether their work belongs to Circuit / Non-Circuit Track at the time of Registration.
  • Participants are qualified only after the screening round, based on the judgment of the panel.
  • Selected teams would be invited to Jyothi Engineering College to present their project and will be charge a registration fee of Rs.100/-

    • POINTS TO NOTE

  • Teams will be given a space to set up their model/design at the venue for judging.
  • A detailed poster in support of the design and highlighting the important aspects should also be brought.
  • The poster should be printed in photo-paper/vinyl/glossy paper/flex (in A2 Size).
  • The decision of the judges shall be treated as final and binding on all.
  • The organizers reserve the right to change any or all of the above rules as they deem fit.
  • Any change in the rules will be informed and notified to the registered participants via email.
  • The organizers reserve the right to hold back the cash prizes if the project presented does not meet the expectations of the judgment panel.
  • If the team displays false/violent behavior, the participation will be terminated and appropriate action will be taken.

    • PRIZE

  • First Prize :- INR 15000
  • Second Prize:- INR 10000
